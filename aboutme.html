HELLO, I'm Hani.



Hi everyone! My name is Nur Hani Syakinah, and I am currently in the 4th semester student at University Teknologi Mara (UiTM), majoring in Library Informatics . My academic journey has been filled with exciting learning experiences and opportunities to explore my passion for library and information science.

Right now, I'm diving headfirst into the exciting world of my 4th semester, tackling courses like cataloging & classification, multimedia, web content development, libraries & customers, and metadata development. These subjects are fascinating, and web content development, in particular, has really grabbed my attention.

Building my own personal website has become my current project, and I'm absolutely loving the process! It's a fantastic way to combine the knowledge I'm gaining in multimedia and web content creation, while also giving me a platform to showcase myself and my interests.
